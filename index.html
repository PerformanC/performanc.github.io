Our History

2022 — Foundation Born in the aftermath of the COVID-19 pandemic, The PerformanC Organization (TPO) was established with a clear vision: to build and publish high-quality software that prioritizes performance, efficiency, and minimal resource usage. From the very beginning, the organization committed to something deeper than just code, transparency, and accountability at every step of development. First Release — CogLink The first project released under the organization marked its technical direction. CogLink, a LavaLink client written in C99, became the foundation of PerformanC’s identity— lean, efficient, and engineered with precision. Growth — Expanding the Ecosystem As time progressed, PerformanC continued to grow beyond its initial scope. More projects were developed and released, each carrying the same philosophy:
  • performance-first design
  • clean and reliable implementation
  • practical value for real-world use
 Some of these projects began gaining traction, gradually building recognition within the community. Today — A Global Effort Today, PerformanC stands as a collaborative effort formed by: Lucas, Rainy, TehPig, ThePedroo, and Nicolas With 10+ active projects, the organization delivers high-standard software used by individuals and other projects across the globe.
